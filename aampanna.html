Aam Panna

Refreshing indian beverage drink recipe made with raw, sour and tender mangoes and additional spices for additional flavours. it hails from the western india or particularly from the marathi cuisine.

It is made when mangoes make their appearance in the hot Indian summers. Aam panna has a tangy, sweet taste with hints of cardamom, cumin & black salt. The addition of these spice powders and black salt not only brings in more flavours but also helps in the digestion


several health benefits of drinking aam panna

  • It prevents the body from getting heat stroke and keeps it cool.
  • Raw mangoes are rich in vitamin c and iron, plus the addition of jaggery also gives an iron boost.
  • It helps in preventing stomach problems in the summers.
  • Good for digestion.

Ingredients Used in making aam panna are given below


  • Raw Sweet Mango
  • Sugar
  • Salt (Black & White)
  • Pudina
  • Rooted Jeera
  • Lemon

Wash green mangoes and wipe them with a paper towel. Peel them using a knife and chop the flesh into cubes. Discard the seeds.Add the cubed mangoes to the instant pot along with 1 cup of water.Close the lid of the pot and set the valve to the sealing position. Cook the cubed mangoes on very high pressure for 5 to 10 minutes. Open the lid of the pot and let the mixture cool down completely.Transfer the cooked and cooled green mangoes to a blender.Add cup mint leaves, cup of granulated sugar, teaspoon roasted cumin powder, and some amount of black salt to the blender and blend until smooth.Transfer the mango mixture to a large bowl or a pitcher. Add several cups of water and stir well. Refrigerate the panna for a few hours and now your drink is ready.
